Simple Omelette Recipe

Preparation time

  • Total:Approxmately 10 minutes
  • Preparation: 5 mimutes
  • Cooking: 5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • Salt, to taste
  • Pepper, to taste
  • 1 tableston of butter or oil
  • Optional filings: cheese,diced vegatables, cooked meats, herbs

Instructions

  • Beat the eggs:In a bowl, beat the eggs with a pinch of salt and pepper until they are well mixed. You can add a tablespoon of water or milk for a fluffier textture.

  • Heat the pan:Place a non-stick frying pan over medium heat and add butter or oil.
  • Cook the omelette:Once the butter is meleted and bubbling, pur the eggs. Tilt the pan to ensure the eggs evenly coat the surface.
